ABOUT CARRENTAL

What is CarRental?
CarRental is a self drive car rental service that provides you with privacy, fun and convenience without owning a car. Cars are delivered and picked up at your doorstep across all serviceable areas.
Am I eligible to use CarRental services?
You must be at least 21 years old and hold a Light Motor Vehicle driving license that is at least 6 months old. The license must be original or available via DigiLocker. Commercial licenses and scanned copies are not accepted.
Which areas does CarRental serve?
We operate in Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Mumbai, Pune, Delhi NCR, Chandigarh, Jaipur, Visakhapatnam, Mysore, Coimbatore, Mangalore, Kolkata, Kochi, Ahmedabad, Trivandrum, Surat, Tirupati, Vijayawada, Bhubaneswar, Nagpur and Vadodara.
Is there a kilometres limit?
Kilometre limits depend on the selected plan. Extra kilometres are charged as per plan. Unlimited kilometre plans have no distance restriction.
Are there restrictions on where I can travel?
Cars have All India Tourist Permit allowing travel across states. Travel to Leh/Ladakh, Spiti Valley and restricted regions is not allowed. Interstate taxes and tolls are borne by the customer.
Do I have to return the car to the same location?
No. You may choose different pickup and drop locations within the same city if selected during booking.

YOUR BOOKINGS

How do I make a booking?
Bookings can be made via the website or app by selecting city, dates, location, car type and completing payment.
Can I modify or cancel my booking?
Yes. Modification and cancellation depend on timing and availability. Charges may apply.

PAYMENTS & REFUNDS

What payment modes are accepted?
Credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UPI and wallets are accepted.
How long do refunds take?
Refunds are generally processed within 10 days after booking completion.

YOUR TRIP

What documents are required at pickup?
Original driving license and valid ID proof must be shown at the time of car delivery.
What if I leave something in the car?
We will try to help recover lost items if reported quickly, but recovery cannot be guaranteed.
